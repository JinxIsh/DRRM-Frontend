Walk-in Reactivation

  1. Download or Request the Form: Obtain the reactivation form from the DRRM Office (Window D) or download it. Fill out the required information and provide a copy of your passport and Alien Certificate of Registration.
  2. Seek Clearances: Visit the SAO and Accounting Department for clearance.
  3. Reactivate Student Number: Return to Window D for reactivation of your student number.
  4. Proceed with Enrollment: Complete your enrollment via this link

Online Reactivation

  1. Email: Send an email to ienroll.cal@ue.edu.ph.
  2. Fill Out the Form: Complete the form provided and await confirmation from the Officer-in-charge.
  3. Activate Student Number: Once activated, proceed with your enrollment at iEnroll.
  4. Payment & Registration: Pay at the Cashier and print your Registration Invoice at the designated printing area.

For All Active Students: If you cannot sign in to Online Registration due to a pop-up message "See the Dean" or "See the Guidance Counselor", please follow this link.
